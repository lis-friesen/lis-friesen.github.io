Fuzzlord

Fuzzlord was originally called Cookie when he was first born. He was from the same farm as Pocket. He was a mischievous kitten who followed everyone around. When he was about a year old, his behavior quickly shifted into being highly aggressive and unpredictable. He bit a person which sent them to the hospital with a nasty infection and was put down soon after. It is believed this was genetic as his father and brother behaved similarly.
